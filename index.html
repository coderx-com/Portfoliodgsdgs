About Me

I'm Omkar Shashank Pathare — a Data Scientist and ML Engineer pursuing my Master's in Data Science at Stevens Institute of Technology (Expected May 2026), with a Bachelor's in AI & Data Science from Thadomal Shahani Engineering College, Mumbai University (2024, Distinction).

I engineer scalable ML solutions across supply chain, finance, and healthcare — from fine-tuning LLMs with QLoRA to building Azure-native MLOps pipelines and recommendation engines that actually move the needle. My toolkit spans Python, R, SQL, Scikit-learn, TensorFlow, Hugging Face, Tableau, Spark, and Azure.

Outside of data, I'm an F1 fan who occasionally takes it too far — my SARIMA forecasting project on race strategy started as a hobby and turned into one of my favourite pieces of work. I'm also into AI ethics, open-source, and finding patterns in things that aren't supposed to have patterns.
